Our History

SPG Canada is a federal registered company based in Montreal and was established for the purpose of real estate investing and providing professional, proactive and dynamic property management services in the city of Montreal and surrounding areas.

About us

SPG Canada principals and senior staff have over 20 years of combined property management and construction experience in Canada and China. This experience is crucial for the successful and efficient management of our costumer's property. In addition, our superior service, thorough knowledge of building construction, maintenance and repair, sets us apart from our competitors.

Our corporate philosophy is to eliminate the burdens of management while saving our customer's time and money, while providing our professional advice for real estate investing. In fact, we have owned quite few estates in the region of Montreal and Toronto area. As the landlord, we understand deeply the challenges property owners face every day and we have designed our services to ensure the smooth operation of our customer's property in an efficient and effective manner.

At SPG, we focus on our customer and we will customize our services in a manner that best suits their needs.
